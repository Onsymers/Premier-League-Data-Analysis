Investigation Overview¶

The football matches are unpredictable, could we have any chance to know what can affect the results of the matches?
We will investigate about some match stats as:

  • Does playing at home stadium gives you more advantage on the other team?
  • Does number of shots and passes affect the results?} We will see some visualizations which may answer these questions.

Dataset Overview¶

Provide a brief overview of the dataset to be presented here. The dataset is about Premier League from season 2011/12 to season 2019/20,it contains 3327 matches. The dataset is about stats of the matches like passes, shots, possession, attendance.
