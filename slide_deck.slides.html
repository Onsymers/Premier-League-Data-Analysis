Premier League Dataset Explanatory¶

by Omar Farouk¶

Investigation Overview¶

The football matches are unpredictable, could we have any chance to know what can affect the results of the matches?
We will investigate about some match stats as:

  • Does playing at home stadium gives you more advantage on the other team?
  • Does the number of shots affect the results?
  • Does the number of attenance of fans in the match can make differnces in the result of the match? We will see some visualizations which may answer these questions.

Dataset Overview¶

Provide a brief overview of the dataset to be presented here. The dataset is about Premier League from season 2011/12 to season 2019/20,it contains 3327 matches. The dataset is about stats of the matches like passes, shots, possession, attendance.

Wins rate to Home teams and Away teams.¶

The win rate for the home team is 45.5% and the win rate for the away team is 30.3%, the rest is about tie between both teams (24.2%). it is abvoius that the Home team has higher probability than the Away team to win the match at home stadium.
